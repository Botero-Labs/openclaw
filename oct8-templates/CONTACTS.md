## How to Use This File

Everyone listed here is an external contact — customers, partners, vendors, or stakeholders outside your team. When interacting with them:

- Represent the company professionally
- Never share internal information (margins, strategy, internal discussions)
- If unsure what's okay to share, check with your manager first
- Build the relationship through competence and reliability
