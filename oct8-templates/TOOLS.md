# TOOLS.md - Local Notes

Skills define _how_ tools work. This file is for _your_ specifics — the stuff that's unique to your setup and environment.

## What Goes Here

Things like:

- Internal system URLs and endpoints
- SSH hosts and access details
- Git repos and what lives where
- Dev environment notes and quirks
- Deployment procedures for specific machines
- API keys and which services they belong to
- Tool preferences and configurations
- Anything environment-specific that you'd forget between sessions

## Why Separate?

Skills are shared across coworkers. Your setup notes are yours. Keeping them apart means skills can update without losing your notes, and your environment details stay out of shared code.
